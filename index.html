Stereo Vision Camera System
Dual MIPI camera rig for stereo computer vision. Custom mechanical enclosure with multiple latch and clamping mechanism iterations for long-term optical alignment stability. Hardware VSYNC synchronization via RK3588S PWM, FPGA-based frame processing, USB3 UVC output — designed as a driver-free integrated unit.
SolidWorks RK3588S Lattice CrossLink-NX MIPI CSI-2 USB3 UVC Embedded Linux
RK3588S Custom Carrier Board (LubanCat 5)
Full carrier board design for RK3588S SoM. Two-PCB architecture: power board with CN3722 LiHV 2S charger, MAX17044G fuel gauge, SOC LED indicator; carrier board with high-speed routing for USB3, PCIe, and MIPI CSI-2. Full bring-up fault isolation including I2C floating, PCIe CLKREQN kernel hang, and multi-rail sequencing failures.
Altium Designer RK3588S USB-C PD LiHV Battery PCIe DTS Overlay
LattePanda Mu Carrier Board (Intel N150)
Custom carrier board for Intel N150 SoM with USB-C PD negotiation via CH224K, SY8253ADC buck converter, TUSB1064 USB MUX, and FUSB302 CC detection. Debugged PD negotiation stuck at 9V (CFG resistors off by 1000×) and buck EN chicken-and-egg boot sequencing failure.
EasyEDA Intel N150 CH224K FUSB302 USB-C PD
Orange Pi CM5 Carrier Board
Carrier board consolidating dual USB-C functions (BQ25890 charging + FUSB302 PD) into a single UT12113 port. Bidirectional power switching with AO3401A × 2 back-to-back MOSFETs, SuperSpeed ESD protection, and I2C level shifting for touchscreen integration.
BQ25890 FUSB302 USB-C PD RK3588S
